Furries? at MY university!?

It's more likely than you think!

Triton Tails (est. 2021) is a social club with over 400 members, devoted to appreciating and engaging with furry culture and media through a positive and lighthearted lens. Our shared interests bring us together in many forms: art-making, sharing media, watching films/movies, and crafting costumes and cosplay. Our events include game nights, study jams, VR hangouts, and festivals with hundreds of attendees—taking place across San Diego, LA, and beyond. Our extensive alumni network connects students with professionals and employers across industry and academia worldwide. We hope to showcase the best of what the fandom has to offer: a place for community, belonging, and having fun, whether or not you're a furry.

What is a furry?

Furries are anyone who is interested in animal characters, like from video games, books, or shows. Think of Disney's Zootopia, Puss in Boots, and Sonic the Hedgehog. Furries often create their own original characters, called fursonas, which represent them in a fun and creative way.

At its core, being a furry is about having fun, expressing yourself, and connecting with others who share an interest in cool animal characters!

How do I get involved?

We have casual hangouts and study jams almost every day, and club meetups every week. Join our Discord to stay updated and sign-up for events.

For our most active members: We also have club merch and membership cards available, which give you perks and discounts across campus!
